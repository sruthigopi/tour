Artforms

The performing arts of Gods Own Country have a special pull and connection amongst all audiences. On stage or by the road, the setting is never the primary attraction but rather, what steals the spotlight.

Spices have always played an integral part of Kerala's history. Our food is an example of the very same and we have offerings for people from all over the world.
